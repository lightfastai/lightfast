  ★ Insight ─────────────────────────────────────
  The key issue with Dependabot for this repo isn't whether it's enabled — it's
  that Dependabot doesn't understand pnpm catalogs (catalog: entries in your
  pnpm-workspace.yaml). It would create PRs that update individual package.json
  files without touching the catalog, breaking workspace consistency. Renovate
  handles this natively.
