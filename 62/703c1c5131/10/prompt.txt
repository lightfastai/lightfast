## Author quality bar

**Auto-include:**
- 500+ followers AND bio mentions: founder, CTO, eng, builds, ships, AI, agents, dev tools, infra, researcher
- Verified blue + technical content (skip influencer / general-business blue checks)
- Anyone @lightfastai already follows or who follows back

**Auto-skip:**
- <500 followers (unless bio is bullseye ICP ‚Äî *founder of <agent infra startup>*, *eng at <known AI lab>*)
- Bio is generic ("entrepreneur", "investor", "AI enthusiast" with no specifics)
- Last 5 posts are all hashtag-stuffed promo
- Account created in last 90 days with thread-bot posting cadence
- Engagement looks bot-amplified: 100+ likes / 0 replies, identical reply patterns

## Post quality bar

**Engage:**
- Genuine question or working-out-loud thinking about agents/memory/infra/MCP
- Strong opinion grounded in shipped work (not airport-takeoff threads)
- Bug report, frustration, or unmet need in the dev-tool stack ‚Äî Lightfast-shaped problem
- Replies on launch / changelog threads from Anthropic, Cursor, Vercel, Linear, Sentry ‚Äî high-context, builders are watching

**Skip:**
- Pure promotion / launch announcement of an MCP-aggregator / vibes product
- Politics, drama, hot-takes detached from work
- Posts older than 6 hours unless mega-thread (replies feel late after that window)
- Anything that already has 50+ replies ‚Äî your reply gets buried

## Reply rubric ‚Äî Lightfast voice

**Voice:** technical, curious, lowercase ok, dry. Never starts with *"great point"* / *"this"* / *"so true"*. Never pitches Lightfast unprompted. Never asks a bait question to fish for engagement. 1‚Äì10 words, hard cap.

**Good replies (engage):**
- *"persistent state across runs is the unlock"* (peer founder shipped a primitive)
- *"goal-fit signals beat recency every time"* (someone working out memory ranking)
- *"citations on every memory writeback helps"* (someone raising memory-poisoning)
- *"same ‚Äî cold tool calls especially"* (someone complaining about Claude Code latency)

**Bad replies (would not ship):**
- *"great point! we're building exactly this"* (pitchy, sycophantic)
- *"have you tried Lightfast?"* (pitch, ignores the post)
- *"agents need memory to scale, totally agree"* (generic, adds nothing)
- *"interesting take ‚Äî what do you think about XYZ?"* (bait question, low-effort)

**Length test:** if a reply could be a tweet on its own (i.e. has nothing to do with the original post), reject it.

## Scoring + dedupe

For each candidate:
- `icp_fit` (1‚Äì5): how well does the author match Lightfast's ICP?
- `resonance` (1‚Äì5): how directly does the post hit Lightfast's value prop?
- `engagement_signal` (1‚Äì5): is the post fresh + getting traction (not buried, not bot-amplified)?
- `priority_score` = `icp_fit + resonance + engagement_signal` (max 15)

Dedupe by `author_handle` ‚Äî one opportunity per author per run, even if they have multiple matching posts. Pick their highest-scoring post.
