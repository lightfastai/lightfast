## Overview

Lightfast's Intercom integration will ingest your Intercom activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Intercom events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Conversation lifecycle and assignments
- Replies, notes, and tag changes
- User and contact profile updates
- Article views and help center activity

## Why it matters

Intercom produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.

Planned — join the waitlist to be notified when Intercom is live.
