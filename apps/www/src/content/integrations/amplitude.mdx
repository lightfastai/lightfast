## Overview

Lightfast's Amplitude integration will ingest your Amplitude activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Amplitude events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Event data across users and sessions
- Cohort membership and audience changes
- Experiment exposure and results
- Dashboard and alert updates

## Why it matters

Amplitude produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
