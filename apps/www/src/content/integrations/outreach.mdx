## Overview

Lightfast's Outreach integration will ingest your Outreach activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Outreach events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Sequence state changes and step completion
- Task creation and assignments
- Call logs and outcomes
- Opportunity and account updates

## Why it matters

Outreach produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
