## Overview

Lightfast's Notion integration will ingest your Notion activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Notion events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Page creates, edits, and archives
- Database row changes and schema edits
- Block-level content updates
- Comment activity and mentions

## Why it matters

Notion produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.

Planned — join the waitlist to be notified when Notion is live.
