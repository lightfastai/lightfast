## Overview

Lightfast's Cal.com integration will ingest your Cal.com activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Cal.com events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Booking lifecycle: created, rescheduled, canceled
- Event type and availability edits
- Team membership and routing changes
- Meeting recording and survey events

## Why it matters

Cal.com produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
