## Overview

Lightfast's Instantly integration will ingest your Instantly activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Instantly events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Campaign sends and reply activity
- Lead list imports and enrichments
- Deliverability and inbox warmup metrics
- Sequence step performance

## Why it matters

Instantly produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
