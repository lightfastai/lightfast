## Overview

Lightfast's Apollo integration will ingest your Apollo activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Apollo events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Sequence step sends and replies
- Prospect and account list updates
- Meeting bookings and call logs
- Email engagement metrics

## Why it matters

Apollo produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
