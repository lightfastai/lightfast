## Overview

Lightfast's Neon integration will ingest your Neon activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Neon events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Branch create, merge, and delete events
- Compute suspend and resume activity
- Query performance insights
- Project and role configuration changes

## Why it matters

Neon produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.

Planned — join the waitlist to be notified when Neon is live.
