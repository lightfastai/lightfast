## Overview

Lightfast's Zendesk integration will ingest your Zendesk activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Zendesk events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Ticket creation, updates, and solves
- Agent replies and internal notes
- Macro usage and triggers
- Satisfaction ratings and tags

## Why it matters

Zendesk produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
