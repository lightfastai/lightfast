## Overview

Lightfast's Jira integration will ingest your Jira activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Jira events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Issue lifecycle: created, updated, transitioned, closed
- Sprint planning and completion
- Comments, mentions, and attachments
- Workflow and board configuration changes

## Why it matters

Jira produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
