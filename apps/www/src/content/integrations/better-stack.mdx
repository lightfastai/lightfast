## Overview

Lightfast's Better Stack integration will ingest your Better Stack activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Better Stack events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Uptime checks: up, down, and degraded
- Heartbeat misses and recoveries
- Incident creation and resolution
- Status page publishing events

## Why it matters

Better Stack produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
