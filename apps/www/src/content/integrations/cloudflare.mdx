## Overview

Lightfast's Cloudflare integration will ingest your Cloudflare activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Cloudflare events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Workers deployments and route changes
- Zone DNS and settings edits
- R2, KV, and D1 resource activity
- WAF and security alerts

## Why it matters

Cloudflare produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
