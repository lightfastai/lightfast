## Overview

Lightfast's Stripe integration will ingest your Stripe activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Stripe events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Customer lifecycle and subscription changes
- Invoice and payment events
- Disputes, refunds, and chargebacks
- Product and price catalog edits

## Why it matters

Stripe produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
