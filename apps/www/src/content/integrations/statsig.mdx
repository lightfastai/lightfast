## Overview

Lightfast's Statsig integration will ingest your Statsig activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Statsig events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Gate and experiment config changes
- Rollout percentage updates
- Experiment results and readouts
- Dynamic config and target audience edits

## Why it matters

Statsig produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
