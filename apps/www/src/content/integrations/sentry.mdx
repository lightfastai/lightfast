## Overview

Lightfast's Sentry integration will ingest your Sentry activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Sentry events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Issue lifecycle: new, regressed, assigned, resolved
- Releases and deploy markers across environments
- Performance transactions and tracing data
- Alert rule triggers and notifications

## Why it matters

Sentry produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
