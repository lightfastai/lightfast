## Overview

Lightfast's Fireflies integration will ingest your Fireflies activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Fireflies events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Meeting transcripts and speaker attribution
- Action item and decision extraction
- Summary and topic tagging
- Integration-synced meeting metadata

## Why it matters

Fireflies produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
