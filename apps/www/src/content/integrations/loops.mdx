## Overview

Lightfast's Loops integration will ingest your Loops activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Loops events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Transactional and loop-based email sends
- Engagement events: opens, clicks, unsubscribes
- Audience segment updates
- Template publishing events

## Why it matters

Loops produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
