## Overview

Lightfast's LaunchDarkly integration will ingest your LaunchDarkly activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate LaunchDarkly events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Flag lifecycle: created, toggled, archived
- Environment-specific targeting rules
- Release progression and kill-switch activations
- Audit log of every flag edit

## Why it matters

LaunchDarkly produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
