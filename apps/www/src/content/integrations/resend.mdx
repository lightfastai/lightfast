## Overview

Lightfast's Resend integration will ingest your Resend activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Resend events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Email sent, delivered, and bounced events
- Opens, clicks, and unsubscribes
- Domain verification and DNS status
- Template and broadcast performance

## Why it matters

Resend produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
