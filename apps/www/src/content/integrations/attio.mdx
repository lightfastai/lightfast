## Overview

Lightfast's Attio integration will ingest your Attio activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Attio events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Record creates, updates, and deletions
- List membership and pipeline changes
- Workflow automation runs
- Notes, tasks, and relationship edits

## Why it matters

Attio produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
