## Overview

Lightfast's PagerDuty integration will ingest your PagerDuty activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate PagerDuty events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Incident lifecycle: trigger, acknowledge, resolve
- Escalation policies and on-call shifts
- Service and team membership changes
- Post-incident reviews and notes

## Why it matters

PagerDuty produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
