## Overview

Lightfast's Typeform integration will ingest your Typeform activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Typeform events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- New form responses and completions
- Partial submissions and drop-off events
- Form and question configuration changes
- Workspace and team updates

## Why it matters

Typeform produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
