## Overview

Lightfast's Customer.io integration will ingest your Customer.io activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Customer.io events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Campaign sends and recipient engagement
- Newsletter and broadcast activity
- Transactional API events
- Segment and audience changes

## Why it matters

Customer.io produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
