## Overview

Lightfast's CircleCI integration will ingest your CircleCI activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate CircleCI events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Workflow runs, job status, and step timing
- Failures, retries, and queue delays
- Deploy jobs across environments
- Contexts, orbs, and config changes

## Why it matters

CircleCI produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
