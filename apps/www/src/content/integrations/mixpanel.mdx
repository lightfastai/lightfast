## Overview

Lightfast's Mixpanel integration will ingest your Mixpanel activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Mixpanel events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Event streams and user profiles
- Funnel and retention cohort data
- Project and dashboard edits
- Alert rules and insight snapshots

## Why it matters

Mixpanel produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
