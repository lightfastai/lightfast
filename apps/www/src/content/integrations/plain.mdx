## Overview

Lightfast's Plain integration will ingest your Plain activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Plain events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Thread lifecycle and assignment changes
- Customer messages and internal notes
- Labels, tiers, and custom fields
- Linear and GitHub cross-references

## Why it matters

Plain produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
