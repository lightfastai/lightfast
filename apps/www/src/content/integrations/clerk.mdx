## Overview

Lightfast's Clerk integration will ingest your Clerk activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Clerk events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- User signups, sign-ins, and sign-outs
- Session and MFA activity
- Organization and membership changes
- Webhook and API key edits

## Why it matters

Clerk produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
