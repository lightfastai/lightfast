## Overview

Lightfast's Supabase integration will ingest your Supabase activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Supabase events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Database row changes via realtime
- Auth signups and sign-ins
- Edge function invocations and errors
- Storage uploads and policy edits

## Why it matters

Supabase produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
