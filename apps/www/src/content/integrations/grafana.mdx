## Overview

Lightfast's Grafana integration will ingest your Grafana activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Grafana events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Dashboard changes and folder updates
- Alert rule state transitions
- Grafana OnCall shifts and escalations
- Annotations tied to deploys or incidents

## Why it matters

Grafana produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.

Planned — join the waitlist to be notified when Grafana is live.
