## Overview

Lightfast's incident.io integration will ingest your incident.io activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate incident.io events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Incident declarations, severity changes, and closures
- Follow-up actions and assignments
- Post-incident reviews and timelines
- On-call schedule and responder data

## Why it matters

incident.io produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
