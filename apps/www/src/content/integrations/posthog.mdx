## Overview

Lightfast's PostHog integration will ingest your PostHog activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate PostHog events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Product events and autocapture data
- Feature flag evaluations and changes
- Session replay markers and cohorts
- Insight and dashboard updates

## Why it matters

PostHog produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
