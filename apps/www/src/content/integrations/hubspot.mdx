## Overview

Lightfast's HubSpot integration will ingest your HubSpot activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate HubSpot events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Deal stage changes and close events
- Contact and company record updates
- Email, call, and meeting activity
- Marketing campaign engagement

## Why it matters

HubSpot produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
