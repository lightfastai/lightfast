## Overview

Lightfast's Slack integration will ingest your Slack activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Slack events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Channel messages, threads, and reactions
- User mentions and keyword triggers
- Channel and workspace membership changes
- File shares and pinned items

## Why it matters

Slack produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
