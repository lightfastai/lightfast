## Overview

Lightfast's Segment integration will ingest your Segment activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Segment events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Track, identify, and page events from all sources
- User and group trait updates
- Audience membership changes
- Source and destination configuration edits

## Why it matters

Segment produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
