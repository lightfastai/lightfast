## Overview

Lightfast's Datadog integration will ingest your Datadog activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Datadog events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Monitor alerts across environments
- Log events and aggregates
- Service checks and synthetics
- Incident markers and events

## Why it matters

Datadog produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
