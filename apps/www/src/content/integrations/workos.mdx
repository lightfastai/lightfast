## Overview

Lightfast's WorkOS integration will ingest your WorkOS activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate WorkOS events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- SSO sign-in and session events
- SCIM user and group provisioning
- Directory sync activity
- Audit log events for enterprise admins

## Why it matters

WorkOS produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.

Planned — join the waitlist to be notified when WorkOS is live.
