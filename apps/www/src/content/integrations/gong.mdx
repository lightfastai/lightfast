## Overview

Lightfast's Gong integration will ingest your Gong activity into a single event stream alongside GitHub, Vercel, and Linear. Ask questions across the data and let Lightfast correlate Gong events with the rest of your engineering graph.

## What we'll ingest

- Call recordings, transcripts, and topics
- Deal health and pipeline signals
- Account and opportunity updates
- Coaching and scorecard events

## Why it matters

Gong produces signals that matter to engineering, product, and revenue teams. Lightfast brings those signals into the same graph as your code and planning data — so one question can reach across every tool your team uses.
